03 — About the studio

A small studio.
Uncommonly
intentional.

Atelier Forma is a Warsaw-based practice of eight designers, architects, and strategists. We take on a deliberate number of projects each year — enough to give each one the attention it deserves, no more. Our clients find us through word of mouth, and most stay for years.

We believe the most enduring work comes from patience: listening before proposing, questioning before designing, and refining until there is nothing left to remove.

05 How we work
I
Listen

Every engagement begins with sustained, generous listening. We seek to understand not just what you want, but why — and what you haven't yet thought to ask for.

II
Define

Clarity is the first deliverable. We codify ambitions into a clear creative brief that aligns all stakeholders and becomes the north star for every decision ahead.

III
Craft

We work slowly and deliberately, in close collaboration. Concepts are developed, challenged, and refined through rigorous critique and honest dialogue.

IV
Endure

Our work does not stop at handover. We remain close partners — ensuring that what we build together ages with grace and continues to serve its purpose.
